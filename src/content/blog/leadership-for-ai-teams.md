AI teams need speed, sure. They also need standards. Both or bust.

![Leadership for AI Teams](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1521737604893-d14cc237f11d?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1600&q=80)

What actually works:

- **Make quality non-negotiable.** If the bar is fuzzy, quality dies.
- **Prefer reversible decisions.** Save the drama for the hard calls.
- **Align vocab across teams.** Product, design, and engineering should mean the same thing by the same words.
- **Reward learning velocity.** Smart iteration beats ego shipping.

When these are true, you move fast *and* stay sane.
