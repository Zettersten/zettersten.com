AI teams need both speed **and** standards.

A few principles I lean on:

- Keep quality bars explicit and visible.
- Make decisions reversible whenever possible.
- Build shared vocabulary across product, design, and engineering.
- Reward learning velocity, not just output volume.

When these are in place, you can move fast without accumulating chaos.
